Booking Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your new home in Central Park.

How it works:

Step 1

Rental Application

Download and fill out the official application form to start the process.

Application Form
Step 2

Personal Identification

Provide a clear copy of your valid ID or Passport for verification purposes.

Step 3

Supporting Documents

Proof of Employment (40x monthly rent) OR University Acceptance Letter + Guarantor Form.

Step 4

Lease Agreement

Once approved, we will send you the lease and rider agreement to be signed.

Step 5

Deposit Payment

$500 booking deposit for short stays. First and last month's rent for longer leases.

Step 6

Move In

You're all set! Collect your keys and welcome to your new home in NYC.
